Required Documents for Loan Waiver

  • Aadhar Card: Proof of identity & residence.
  • Farmer Smart Card: Verification of farmer status.
  • Loan Agreement: Proof of loan taken.
  • Land Ownership Proof: Documents proving land ownership.
  • Bank Loan Statement: Loan repayment history.
  • Government Approval: Any government-issued loan waiver certificates.
